📄 Privacy Policy

SocialCore Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SocialCore ("we", "our", "us") collects information you provide when using our social media campaign management platform. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you register or sign in via OAuth providers (Google, Facebook, Instagram, Auth0).
  • Social Media Data: When you connect social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn), we collect access tokens, profile names, page IDs, and organization IDs necessary for posting content on your behalf.
  • Content: Social media post drafts, comments, feedback, and media attachments you create within the platform.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the platform, workspace activity, and feature usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SocialCore platform
  • To schedule and publish social media content on connected accounts (only with your explicit authorization)
  • To enable team collaboration, comments, and approval workflows
  • To improve our AI-powered content review and suggestion features using Google Gemini
  • To communicate important updates, security alerts, and service changes

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL with Row-Level Security) and encrypted in transit via HTTPS. Authentication tokens are JWT-based with expiration. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted database storage
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) ensuring workspace-scoped data access
  • Short-lived OAuth tokens with refresh mechanisms
  • Regular security audits

4. Third-Party Services

SocialCore integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication backend
  • Google Gemini AI: AI-powered content review and optimization
  • Auth0: Social login authentication (Google, Facebook, Instagram)
  • Facebook Graph API: Publishing content to Facebook pages and Instagram business accounts
  • LinkedIn API: Publishing content to LinkedIn profiles and organization pages
  • Redis (BullMQ): Background job queue for scheduled publishing

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We only share data necessary for the specific service to function.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete a workspace or account, associated data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Backup data may be retained for up to 90 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (see User Data Deletion below)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. See our Data Deletion Instructions page for the full process. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Cookies

SocialCore uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Children's Privacy

SocialCore is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
