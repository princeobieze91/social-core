🗑️ Data Deletion

User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

SocialCore respects your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your posts, comments, workspaces, connected social accounts, and personal information will be permanently removed. This action cannot be reversed.

Option 1: Self-Service Deletion (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your data is directly within the application:

1
Log in to your SocialCore account

Open the SocialCore application and sign in with your credentials.

2
Navigate to your profile

Click on your avatar/profile icon in the sidebar to access your account settings.

3
Request Account Deletion

Select "Delete Account" option and confirm your decision. You will be asked to verify your identity.

4
Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address. Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days.

Option 2: Email Deletion Request

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request via email:

  1. Send an email to social-core@inbound.devmail.email with the subject line DATA DELETION REQUEST
  2. Include the email address associated with your SocialCore account
  3. For security, we may ask you to verify account ownership by responding to a verification email
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send confirmation once deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

  • User profile information (name, email, avatar, role)
  • All workspace memberships and associated roles
  • All social media post drafts, scheduled posts, and published content records
  • All post attachments, comments, feedback, and activity logs
  • Connected social media channel tokens and credentials
  • Authentication records and session data

What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymized analytics: Aggregated, non-personal usage statistics may be retained for platform improvement
  • Legal/Compliance: Data required by applicable law may be retained for the legally mandated period
  • Backup archives: Encrypted backup data may persist for up to 90 days before being overwritten

Data Deletion for Facebook Users

If you connected your Facebook account to SocialCore and wish to have your Facebook-related data deleted:

  • Follow the steps above to request account deletion
  • Your Facebook access tokens, page IDs, and profile information obtained via Facebook Login will be deleted
  • Note: Deleting your SocialCore account does not delete content published to Facebook via SocialCore. To remove published posts, you must do so directly through Facebook's platform.

Processing Time

We process data deletion requests within 30 days of verification. You will be notified via email once the deletion is complete. If you have questions about the status of your request, contact us at social-core@inbound.devmail.email.
